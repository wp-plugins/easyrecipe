Want more traffic to your blog?

Fooderific.com is a food photo gallery created by the folks who brought you EasyRecipe.

It showcases food photos and recipe snippets from food bloggers around the world

It only shows basic recipe details so readers have to visit your blog to read the recipe.

End result? You'll get more traffic!

What's better is that it's all automatic. There's no manual submission required like Foodgawker or Tastespotting.

Your posts are submitted automatically and will appear on Fooderific within a minutes of being published.

Enable Fooderific
Fooderific is enabled! There's just one more step.

Fooderific needs to run a site scan so it can find and include all your current recipe posts.

Once it's completed, EasyRecipe will automatically update Fooderific whenever you add or change a post.

The whole process is automatic and runs in the background so it won't affect your site performance.

Start Site Scan
Great!

A background site scan has been scheduled and will start in just a few seconds.

This tells Fooderific about all your current posts. Once completed, EasyRecipe will keep Fooderific updated automatically.

The scan usually only takes a minute or two to complete, but it may take Fooderific anywhere from a few minutes to several hours to list all your current posts. It depends on how many posts you have and how many other sites have posts waiting to be listed.

Everything happens in the background so you don't need to hang about on this page.

Although it can take quite a while for all your posts to be listed, the first posts should start appearing on Fooderific in just a few minutes.
